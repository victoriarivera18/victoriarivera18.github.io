Qless

Qless is an app idea I began working on during the Spring of 2020. I was tired of getting to certain dining locations on campus and seeing a massive line out the door. What Qless is, is an app that shows users how busy a dining location is in real time. In order to solve this problem, I began brainstorimg with a couple of my friends and we have focused on possible pressure or heat sensors, or facial recogition cameras as solutions. Orginally meant for college students, we have talked about the possibility of branching out to different markets. As of May 25, 2020 we are still finalizing a concrete solution before coding anything.
